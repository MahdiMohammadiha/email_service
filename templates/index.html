Email service

A simple email service built with Flask and MongoDB. Easily send and view emails using a lightweight Flask application backed by MongoDB for storage. Perfect for small-scale email handling needs or as a starting point for larger projects.

README

Welcome to Our Email Service

Our email service is a simple and efficient platform designed to help you manage your email communication effectively. Below is a quick guide to get you started:

  1. Signup: If you're new here, start by signing up for an account. Click on the Signup link and fill in the required information, including your desired username, password, and email address. Once you've completed the signup process, you'll be redirected to the signin page.

  2. Signin: Simply signin with your username and password in Signin.

  3. Compose and Send Emails: Once you're signed in, you can compose and send emails to your contacts. Click on the Send link to create a new email. Enter the recipient's email address, subject, and the content of your email, then click Send.

  4. Check Your Inbox: Want to see if you've received any new emails? Navigate to the Inbox section to view all your incoming emails. You can see the sender's name, the subject of the email, and a preview of the content.

  5. View Sent Emails: Curious about the emails you've sent? Head over to the Sent section to see a list of all the emails you've sent. You can review the recipients, subject, and content of each sent email.

  6. Signout: Finished using our email service? Don't forget to sign out of your account to ensure the security of your information. Click on the Signout link to log out of your account.

That's it! You're all set to start using our email service. If you have any questions or need further assistance, don't hesitate to reach out to our support team in link below. Happy emailing!
